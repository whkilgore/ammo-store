About Our Company

Tier 1 Operator Supply Co. was founded by shooting enthusiasts and former competitive marksmen who wanted to provide reliable range gear at affordable prices. Our mission is simple: supply high‑quality ammunition and tactical equipment to responsible shooters across the United States.

We specialize in bulk range ammunition, tactical accessories, and dependable equipment for training, recreational shooting, and professional use.
